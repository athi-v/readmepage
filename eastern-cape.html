ABOUT Eastern CAPE

Lying on thesouth-eastern South African coast, is a region of great natural beauty,particularly the rugged cliffs, rough seas and dense green bush of the stretchknown as the Wild Coast. In the Eastern Cape, various floral habitats meet.Along the coast, the northern tropical forests intermingle with the moretemperate woods of the south.The province is serviced by airportssituated in Port Elizabeth, East London, Mthatha and Bhisho.
