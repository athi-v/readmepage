About us

Cristal Realty SA is a luxury buyers agency set up in Johannesburg, South Africa, in 2018. Our main objective is representing the purchaser’s interest in a real estate transaction. We guide our clients through the entire buying process, from identifying their perfect home to the conclusion of the agreement.

Our agents are dedicated to protecting the interests of our clients. We have extensive knowledge of the different suburbs in different provinces across the country, so we are best placed to guide you in making the best investment decision. We hold your hand throughout the process, set up tours, help make offers, negotiate with the seller, and refer you to other professionals to ensure you get the best value for your property.

Benefits of using a buyer’s agency

A real estate transaction involves one agent, the listing agent, who represents the seller's interests, leaving no one to represent the party spending the money in this process. And this is where we come in, as the buying agent. We take care of the unbalanced process of buying the property, saving you from the time-consuming process of going through different websites trying to find your ideal property.
